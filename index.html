About me

If someone asked me to describe myself in three words, I would say that I am pragmatic, motiovated, and a hardcore nerd. OK, so that's technically four words, but the 'hardcore' was required.

I am one part researcher, one part gamer, and one part developer. I received my MSc in psychology from the University of Limerick in 2016 and proceeded to complete a PhD in psychology in 2019. I am currently a computer science MSc student with the hopes of leaving the world of academia for the software industry. I utilise this psychological background not only to discern the most important aspects of video games, but also to apply this knowledge to my own software development.
